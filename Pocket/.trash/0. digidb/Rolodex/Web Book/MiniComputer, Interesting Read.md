# Digital Equipment Corporation's PDP-8

> [!quote] PDP-8  
> The world's 1st minicomputer. The 12-bit PDP-8 contained a single 12-bit accumulator (AC), a 1-bit "Link" (L) & a 12-bit program counter (PC)
